Magical Egg Salad

Back to Recipes

Description

Creamy egg salad with a little tang and a Seceret ingredient.

ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon sweet pickle relish
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh chives (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon of steak sauce (secret ingredient)

Directions

  1. Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, then cover, remove from heat, and let sit for 10-12 minutes.
  2. Drain the eggs and cool them under cold running water. Peel the eggs and chop them into small pieces.
  3. In a bowl, combine mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, sweet pickle relish, salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, and steak sauce. Mix well.
  4. Add the chopped eggs to the bowl and gently fold until well combined. If using chives, fold them in as well.
  5. Serve on bread or crackers or as a salad topping.
