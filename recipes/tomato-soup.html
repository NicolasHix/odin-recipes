Tomato Soup

Back to Recipes

Description

This tomato soup recipe is simple, quick, and perfect to make when tomatoes are ripe in gardens and farmers' markets for a delicious summertime treat. Delicious with garlic bread or a grilled cheese sandwich.

Ingrediants

  • 4 cups chopped fresh tomatos
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 4 cloves fresh garlic
  • 1 large slice of onion
  • 2 tblsp butter
  • 2 tblsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp white sugar
  • salt to tatse.

Steps

  1. In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped tomatoes, garlic, and onion. Cook until the tomatoes are soft and the onion is translucent, about 10 minutes.
  2. Stir in the flour and cook for another 2 minutes.
  3. Add the chicken broth and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and blend the soup until smooth using an immersion blender or a regular blender.
  5. Stir in sugar and salt to taste. Serve hot.
