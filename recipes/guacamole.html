Easy Guacamole

Back to Recipes

Description

This easy guacamole recipe is perfect for parties or a quick snack. Made with ripe avocados, lime juice, and a few simple ingredients, it's creamy, tangy, and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small tomato, diced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped cilantro (optional)

Directions

  1. Cut the avocados in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  2. Use a fork to mash the avocados to your desired level of creaminess.
  3. Add the lime juice, chopped onion, diced tomato, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Mix well to combine.
  4. Adjust seasoning to taste. If desired, fold in chopped cilantro for added flavor.
  5. Serve immediately with tortilla chips, or cover with plastic wrap pressed directly onto the surface of the guacamole to prevent browning. Enjoy!
